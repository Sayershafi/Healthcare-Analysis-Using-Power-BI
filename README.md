Interactive Healthcare Analytics Dashboard built using Power BI to analyze emergency room patient flow, waiting times, departmental referrals, and demographic patterns.

---

# Executive Summary

I developed a **Hospital Emergency Room Power BI Dashboard** to help healthcare administrators monitor emergency department performance and patient flow.

The dashboard analyzes patient visits, waiting times, satisfaction scores, admission status, and department referrals. It provides a comprehensive overview of emergency room operations, helping healthcare providers identify bottlenecks, improve patient experience, and optimize resource allocation.

The dataset covers **April 2023 – October 2024** and records **9,216 unique patients** visiting the emergency room.

---

# Business Problem

Emergency rooms often face high patient volumes and long waiting times. Without proper analytics, hospital administrators struggle to understand patient patterns, peak hours, and departmental workload.

Healthcare managers need an analytical dashboard that answers questions such as:

- How many patients visit the emergency room?
- What is the average patient waiting time?
- Which departments receive the most referrals?
- Which age groups visit the emergency room most frequently?
- What are the busiest days and hours?

Without centralized reporting, identifying operational inefficiencies becomes difficult.

# KPI Summary

- **Total Patients:** 9,216  
- **Average Waiting Time:** 35.3 minutes  
- **Patient Satisfaction Score:** 4.99 / 10  
- **Patients Referred:** 3,816  
- **Patients Admitted:** 4,612  
- **Patients Released:** 4,604  

---

# Key Insights

- The emergency department handled **9,216 patient visits** within 19 months.
- The **average waiting time is approximately 35 minutes**, indicating potential opportunities to improve patient flow.
- Nearly **50% of patients were admitted**, while the rest received treatment and were released.
- **General Practice** received the highest number of referrals, followed by **Orthopedics and Physiotherapy**.
- The **30–39 age group** recorded the highest number of emergency visits.
- **Mondays and weekends** show the highest patient volumes.
- Peak patient arrival hours occur during **late morning and evening periods**.

---

# Business Recommendations

- Improve staffing during **peak hours and high-traffic days**.
- Optimize triage and patient flow to reduce waiting times.
- Allocate more resources to **high-demand departments** such as General Practice and Orthopedics.
- Monitor patient satisfaction scores to enhance service quality.
- Use data-driven dashboards for real-time emergency department management.
